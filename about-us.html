About Us

HelpAge Sri Lanka is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the quality of life for senior citizens across Sri Lanka. Established in 1984, we have been at the forefront of advocating for the rights and well-being of the elderly population. Our mission is to empower older people to lead dignified, active, and healthy lives through various programs and initiatives focused on healthcare, social inclusion, and economic security.

Chairman's Message

"Resilience and Compassion in Challenging Times: A Message from the Chairman of HelpAge Sri Lanka"

2021/2022 was no easy year for HelpAge Sri Lanka. The Corona Virus had, and still continues to have an impact on every aspect of our daily lives and our social lives, leaving traces of adverse consequences.

Despite the COVID shocks, I’m heartened to note that HASL has performed remarkably well and better in the year under review. The performance of service delivery and economic wellbeing has outnumbered the achievements of 2019/2020, the pre-COVID period.

Executive Director's Message

“Four Decades of Dedicated Service to Sri Lanka’s Elderly – A Message from the Executive Director”

It is with great pleasure that I pen this message to the HelpAge Website for information of the viewers.

The first World Assembly on Ageing held in Vienna in 1982 involving member states of the United Nations, specialized agencies, and other concerned agencies focused on the need to have worldwide attention to the problems associated with ageing to launch an international action programme to guarantee economic and social security of the elderly while providing them opportunities to contribute to society. This led to the formulation of 62 policy recommendations involving research, training, education, health, nutrition, social welfare, housing, income security, and protection of the elderly.
